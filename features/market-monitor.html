Feature

Travis Market Monitor

Once a position is on, the thesis has stop conditions. Market Monitor watches the underlying, not the option premium — alerts when the spot, the time decay, or a scheduled catalyst says the thesis changed. Premarket + real-time + end-of-day digests.

👁️
Watches
Spot, not premium
Per-position triggers anchored to the underlying price + time/event, not option mid. Spreads, IV crush, bid-ask noise don't generate false alarms. The underlying is the clean signal.
⚡
Alerts
Real-time, only when it matters
Throttled to market hours plus a configurable vega window before each close. One Telegram ping per meaningful breach — not a stream of tick updates.
🌅
Premarket + EOD
Digests on your schedule
Morning: overnight gap, futures snapshot, catalysts on today's docket. Evening: close vs. entry, IV shift, any watchlist names that moved into/out of trigger zones.
📡
Signal harvest
Insider buys + unusual flow
SEC Form 4 filings matched against your watchlist — unexpected insider buys surface fast. Unusual options activity from public feeds, de-noised and cross-referenced.
Your part
Set the thesis. Let the agent watch it for you.
Not investment advice

Market Monitor surfaces public data and pre-configured alert thresholds. It is not a recommendation service and does not take actions on your account.

Alert data can be delayed, incorrect, or missing. Trading decisions are yours alone; Travis does not execute trades without explicit approval. Past performance does not predict future results.

Real-time monitoring runs during market hours only.
