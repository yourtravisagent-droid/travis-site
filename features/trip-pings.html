Feature

Travis Trip Pings

Most travel apps remind you about the trip itself. Travis reminds you about the hundred small things that need to happen before it — reservations that open exactly 30 days out, packing lists that depend on the kid, passports that expire, restaurants that book up.

🗓️
Windows open
At the exact right moment
Restaurants that release 30 days out, hotel award windows that drop at 11 months, airline upgrade lists that open at T-24h — Travis pings you on the day, not after.
🧳
Packs smart
Context-aware lists
Beach trip? Reef shoes for the kid. Mountain? Altitude meds. Cold? Wool base layers for the partner. Lists derived from destination + season + who's going, not a generic template.
✈️
Watches your itinerary
Changes flagged live
Flight delay, gate change, equipment swap, seat reassignment — the airline's email gets digested, the meaningful bit surfaces. Itinerary stays live, not copy-pasted once and forgotten.
🏨
Pre-arrival
Concierge emails, drafted
Airport transfer requests, late check-in notices, dietary notes, crib/rollaway asks — drafts land in your outbox a week out, ready for one edit then send. You decide when to hit go.
Your part
Book the trip. Let Travis handle the hundred small things.
Pairs with Travel Toolkit (booking) and Calendar Auto-Add (scheduling).
