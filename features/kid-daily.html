Feature

Travis Kid Daily

Daycare and preschool end-of-day reports are a wall of text. Travis reads them for you, surfaces what actually happened, and sends the family group a 60-second summary instead. Lunch picks, school event RSVPs, and teacher notes all land in the same pipeline.

📝
Reads
The daily classroom report
Teachers send structured updates at pickup time. Travis catches the email, extracts: what they ate and how much, nap length, mood, who they played with, any incidents.
💬
Summarizes
Family group, one message
A short, warm recap in the family Telegram. Flags anything worth following up on with the teacher. No classroom jargon — executive-summary tone.
🍽️
Picks lunch
For tomorrow, from the right menu
Scans the school's lunch portal, picks based on your kid's observed favorites, skips travel / holiday days from the calendar. Posts the pick to the family group for a nod.
🎉
Catches events
Birthdays, field trips, picture day
Classmate birthday invites go into the gift-reminders pipeline. Picture day gets added to the calendar with a "pack a change of clothes" note. School breaks surface in trip planning.
Your part
Read the summary over dinner. Nod to the lunch pick.
Works with school portals that email daily reports.
