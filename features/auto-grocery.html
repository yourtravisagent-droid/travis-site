Travis Auto-Grocery

An agent that learns your household's grocery rhythm. Watches what you buy, when you run out, how often it repeats — then runs your weekly Costco, Whole Foods, and Sprouts orders on autopilot. One Telegram tap when a decision is needed. That's your whole job.

🧠
Learns
Your cadence, automatically
A nightly job pulls fresh receipts, infers replenishment cycles, auto-promotes new recurring items into the tracked list after a few observations. No lists to maintain.
🛒
Fills the carts
One pass, three stores
Replenishments due + shared shopping-list extras + meal-plan ingredients, routed to the right store. Travel-aware — skips weeks you're away.
💬
Asks once
Approve / reschedule / skip
Telegram DM with the full item list, subtotal, and a few taps. Reminders fire on a gentle cadence. Unapproved runs auto-skip with a notice so nothing goes out you didn't okay.
✅
Orders itself
Submit, confirm, deliver
Applies don't-substitute per item, picks the chosen window, clicks Place Order. Price-drift abort if the total swings past a threshold. Duplicate-tap guard. Confirmation written back.
Your part
One tap. Optional. Miss it, nothing breaks.
Part of the Travis household suite.
