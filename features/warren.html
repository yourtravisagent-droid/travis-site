Feature

Travis Warren

A committee of specialist agents that pressure-tests every speculative trade before you commit capital. Seven voices — technicals, unusual options flow, catalysts, sentiment, backtest, a dedicated skeptic, and a position sizer — debate it out in under a minute. You get a verdict, a counter-argument, and a sized position. No gut-check trades.

Convenes
Seven specialists on demand
One supervisor, six analysts. technical · UOA · catalyst · sentiment · backtest · skeptic · sizer — each agent narrow, opinionated, accountable to its lens.

Investigates
Live data, every angle
Price / IV / MAs / levels from a TA backend. Unusual options flow, earnings + event calendars, social sentiment, historical analog setups. Ticker-in, evidence-out — no stale snapshots.
⚖️
Debates
Bulls vs. Skeptic
The skeptic's job is to find the failure mode the bulls won't. No trade clears on single-voice enthusiasm. Weighted verdict, explicit dissents — you see the strongest counterargument even when you're right.
💰
Sizes
Risk-scaled, cap-aware
Per-trade caps honored. Strike and expiry aligned to verdict confidence — higher conviction gets closer-dated, lower gets more time. You approve the final click. Travis never routes orders without explicit sign-off.
