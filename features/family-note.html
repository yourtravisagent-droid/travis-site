Travis Family Chief of Staff

A single shared note is the household's operating system. Partner types an errand, kid events get forwarded, birthdays land under the right section. Travis reads it every morning — surfaces deadlines, routes groceries, closes loops automatically.

🗒️
One note
Structured, not scattered
Apple Note with fixed sections: Home TODOs · Kid Events & Gifts · Shopping · Meal Plan · Personal Reminders · Travel Notes · Recurring. Anyone in the household can write. Travis reads in-place.
👀
Watches
Nightly + on-demand
Morning briefing pulls the note fresh. Deadlines surface. New birthdays nudge two weeks + three days out. Travel items cross-referenced against the trip calendar.

Routes

"6× almond milk — Whole Foods" doesn't sit in the note — it flows into the next grocery run, then the bullet gets removed from the note when the cart-add succeeds. Note stays clean.

Kid's birthday party, parents' visit, surgery recovery window — Travis reads dates and adds calendar events with the right guests invited. Two-week reminder for gifts.
