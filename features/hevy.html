Feature

Travis Hevy Review

Most workout apps tell you what you did. Travis tells you whether you're actually getting better. Reads your Hevy history, compares session-to-session against the same slot last cycle, flags regressions before they become a trend.

📊
Compares
Week-over-week, same slot
Today's chest day vs. the last chest day. Weight, reps, RIR side-by-side — not a calendar dump, a meaningful delta. No "today in isolation".
🚨
Flags
Regressions with nuance
Volume down, working-set count down, RIR up — the three signals together. Doesn't cry wolf on a single light bench; waits for the pattern.
🔁
Recognizes
Your cycle structure
12-week min-max blocks, Week 1 intro resets, mid-cycle intensifications, deload weeks. Travis reads the block you're in — week 1 of a build is supposed to feel lighter, not a regression.
💤
Detects deload
Rigorous, not reactive
Requires both volume below threshold and working-set count below trailing average. Single-axis triggers ignored. Catches real recovery weeks, not a low-energy Tuesday.
Your part
Lift. Log in Hevy. Read the evening recap over dinner.
Evening review lands on your schedule. Tone configurable.
