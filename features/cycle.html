Travis Cycle

SoulCycle's booking model is different — each class has its own window, opening about six days out at noon local. Travis watches each one individually, grabs your bike the minute it opens, and tracks which instructors you actually like (so it stops booking the ones you quietly dropped).

🎵
Learns
Your instructor, your bike, your theme
Reads your ride history, ranks instructors by repeat rate, notices the music themes you don't miss. Theme-aware — a one-off special class with a favorite gets prioritized.
⏱️
Watches every class
Per-class opens_at, not a cron
Unlike block-release studios, SoulCycle opens each class on its own schedule. Travis schedules precision timers per class — fires at the minute the window cracks open.
🚲
Books your bike
Position + preference honored
Preferred bike number requested. If taken, next-best from your historical pattern. If the class itself is full, joins waitlist and tracks position with daily re-checks.
🔄
Releases gracefully
13-hour cancel window, respected
Can't make it? Tell Travis, it releases your seat before the penalty window so a waitlisted rider gets it. No no-show fees, no guilt.
