Feature

Travis Wellness Concierge

Medical + wellness appointments are scattered across portals, emails, and text reminders you miss. Travis pulls them into a single pipeline — pre-procedure prep, day-of reminders, post-op care, recurring cleaning cadences — for everyone in the household.

📆
Catches
Every appointment, every portal
Dentist, dermatologist, PCP, specialist, employer-wellness programs, kid's pediatrician. Emails forwarded or filters set, Travis reads and normalizes into a single appointments view.
⏳
Prep pings
At T-2, T-1, day-of
Two days out: "is this still good, need to re-pay co-pay?" Day before: "fast from midnight, bring insurance card." Morning of: "leave by 9:15 for 10 am." Tailored per appointment type.
🩺
Procedure care
Pre- and post-op windows
Surgery scheduled? Antibiotic start timer, soft-food diet rules, ride-home reminder, OTF pause, follow-up appointment at T+14d. Post-op window ends, Travis unpauses fitness and nudges next checkup.
🔁
Cadence
3-month cleanings, annual physicals
Rolling windows — dental at 3 or 4 months, physicals annually, eye exams biennially. When a provider's booking window opens, Travis nudges to grab the slot. Slips-through-cracks, zero.
Your part
Go to the appointment. Travis handles the everything-else.
Multi-person. Honors privacy per family member.
