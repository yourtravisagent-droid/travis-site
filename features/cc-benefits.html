Feature

Travis CC Benefits Tracker

Premium cards come with credits you're paying for — airline incidental, hotel credit, dining stipend, streaming bundle, annual fee offsets. They expire. You forget. Travis tracks what's used and what's about to expire, tiered by urgency, surfaced in the morning briefing.

📊
Catalogs
Every card, every perk
Your card portfolio → the specific credits attached to each. Monthly, quarterly, annual, semi-annual. Dining, airline, hotel, streaming, Uber, wholesale clubs.
📥
Reads usage
From statements + the loyalty sync
Card statements flow in via the inbox scanner. Loyalty-wallet sync shows current balance. Travis reconciles: is this credit burned, partial, untouched?
🎯
Ranks urgency
Urgent · Soon · Planning
Within two weeks of expiry → urgent, top of briefing. Within ~6 weeks → soon. Further out → planning. Tiered nudges so you act on what matters, not on what's noisy.
💡
Suggests
Ways to burn unused credit
"$100 airline credit expires Dec 31 — add onboard wifi on your next flight." "Dining credit — which restaurant on this card's portal you've liked before." Contextual, not generic.
Your part
Skim the briefing. Burn the credit. Annual fees pay themselves.
Pairs with AwardWallet sync for balance truth.
