Feature

Travis Calendar Auto-Add

Forward an Evite, paste a reservation, mention a doctor's appointment — Travis adds it to your Google Calendar with the right guests, the right notes, and the right reminder cadence. The calendar maintains itself.

📨
Catches
Invites from any surface
Forwarded email, note bullet, Telegram message, text receipt from a restaurant confirmation. Travis pulls dates, times, addresses, headcounts.
🧠
Resolves
Recurring vs one-off
Recognizes weekly fitness slots, monthly appointments, school breaks. Attaches the right guests — both partners for household events, just one for personal appointments.
📅
Writes
To your actual calendar
Through the Google Calendar MCP, not a Travis-only shadow calendar. Uses your primary, your partner's email gets invited, location geocoded, notes preserved.
🔔
Reminds
At the right intervals
Two-weeks-out for kid events (gift buying), day-before for restaurants, same-morning for appointments. Context-aware cadence — not one-size-fits-all.
Your part
Forward. Done.
Works with your existing Google Calendar setup.
