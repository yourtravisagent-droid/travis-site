Travis Household Routines

The invisible-but-essential stuff: the cleaner comes Friday, pet meds go in on the 1st, a batch of dog food covers three weeks, a forgotten streaming subscription is renewing tomorrow. Travis keeps the calendar honest across chores, pets, and subscriptions.

🧹
Chores
Recurring with context
Cleaner day reminders ("prep sheets + clear surfaces" the morning of). Gardener day ("pick up pet waste, unlock the gate"). Trash day, school forms due, annual fire extinguisher checks — all the calendar's routine quietly maintained.
🐕
Pet care
Batched, not one-off
Monthly flea + tick reminders. Vet appointments. Homemade food batches — "last batch was three weeks ago, due this weekend." Grooming cadence. Walks flagged when the weather justifies it.

Subscriptions
Nothing renews by surprise
Reads your card statements, identifies recurring charges, flags the ones you haven't used recently. Annual-fee subs get pinged two weeks before renewal. Cancel-or-keep decisions surface in the morning briefing.
