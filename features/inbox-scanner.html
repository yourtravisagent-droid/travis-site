Feature

Travis Inbox Scanner

Email is data, not a to-do list. Travis reads yours — not to respond, but to turn each kind of message into the right downstream action. Receipts become order history. Confirmations become calendar events. Newsletters get summarized. Noise gets filtered.

🧾
Classifies
Each email, by type
Receipt, travel confirmation, school update, financial statement, newsletter, bank alert, subscription renewal. One read, right bucket.
➡️
Routes
To the right pipeline
Grocery receipt → order history cache. Flight status change → trip itinerary update. Credit card statement → wealth dashboard. Bank alert → fraud-check pipeline.
🗜️
Summarizes
Long emails, short form
Newsletters, portal updates, travel itineraries get compressed to the 2-3 lines you actually need. Only forwards surprises — routine items flow silently into briefings.
🛑
Flags
Only when it matters
High-value fraud alerts, contract deadlines, mis-charged subscriptions, delivery exceptions. Telegram-level urgency only for things that can't wait for the morning briefing.
Your part
Forward once, flow forever.
Runs on your own Gmail via MCP. No inbox leaves your account.
